Welcome to my final project page!

My game, simply put, has a hero that uses a gun to shoot enemies.
The game works on levels and waves, and there are different
enemies on different levels. The goal of each wave is to kill all
the enemies which will trigger the next wave/level. The point of
the game is to make it as far as possible. But realistically,
no one makes it past level 4...
